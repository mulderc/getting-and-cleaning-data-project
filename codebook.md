## Identififiers
"SubjectID" [factor 1:30]  ID of the subject who performed the activity, 30 in total
	
"ActivityID" [factor 1:6]  ID of the activity name

 1            WALKING
 2   WALKING_UPSTAIRS
 3 WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
 4            SITTING
 5           STANDING
 6             LAYING
